#La respuesta del estudiante dice que...
(analysis)
**Analyze the Student's Answer:** Start by paraphrasing the student’s response in detail and comment on each statement.
**Rewriting the Information:** Rephrase the information provided in the question to assess whether it is correctly addressed by the student's answer. Check if the response includes new and relevant information.
**Completeness:** Determine if the response is complete, meaning it answers all parts of the question.
**Context Validation:** Use the Knowledge as a reference to validate the accuracy and relevance of the student’s answer. Focus on how well the response aligns with the question asked.
