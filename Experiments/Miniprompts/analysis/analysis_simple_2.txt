#La respuesta del estudiante dice que...
(analysis)
**Analyze the Response:** Begin by paraphrasing the student's answer in detail. Evaluate if the response:
    - Rewrites the information given in the question.
    - Correctly and relevantly answers the question.
    - Is complete, covering all necessary aspects.
    - Uses the Knowledge, if applicable, to validate the accuracy and relevance of the student's response.
    - Shows clear alignment between the question and the provided answer.
